About Us

Ebenezer St. Thomas School, founded in 1993 in Nelamangala, offers quality education through a balanced national and international curriculum. We nurture creativity, leadership, and decision-making in every child. With modern infrastructure and dedicated faculty, we ensure holistic development from pre-primary to Xth grade.

Collaborative Learning Environment

At our school, learning goes far beyond textbooks. We nurture creativity, leadership, and a spirit of adventure through co-curricular programs. Our arts, music, and drama events inspire students to express themselves, while outdoor education builds teamwork and resilience.

Parent Involvement Education

We thrive on collaboration between parents, alumni, and the wider community. Parent workshops on modern education and alumni mentorship programs ensure continuous engagement and shared growth.

Preparing Your Child for the Future

Great teachers create great futures. Our educators receive continuous training and collaborate with global experts to bring the best teaching practices and innovative ideas into our classrooms.
