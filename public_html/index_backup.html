About Us

Ebenezer St. Thomas School

Ebenezer St. Thomas school is the best school in Nelmangala dedicated to delivering quality education with high standards. It,s vibrant and inclusive community founded in 1993. The curriculum is carefully framed by the experts to promote essential skills and knowledge which as per the present global needs. We believe in discovering and nurturing every child's unique talent. Our curriculum is the perfect blend of international as well as national syllabus, which enables every child to explore numerous skills and acquire sufficient knowledge. The teaching technique at Ebenezer St. Thomas School helps the students here to face any kind of challenge and builds decision making ability among them. We provide the best and ideal learning environment with excellent infrastructure to nourish and flourish the future of every student, we take responsibility to take care and nurture students from pre-primary to Xth. Therefore, we ensure our students have an overall development leading to becoming efficient future professionals tomorrow. With a rich history of academic excellence, innovation and service. Our experienced faculty and staff provide a supportive and stimulating environment, fostering creativity, teamwork and leadership through a wide range of extra curricular activities.

Our motto : “In God we trust” guides our mission to empower students to reach their full potential.
