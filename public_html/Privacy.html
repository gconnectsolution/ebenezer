Privacy Policy

At Ebenezer St Thomas School , we prioritize the privacy and security of your personal information. Consent and Updates

You may update, correct, or delete your personal information at any time by contacting us directly. Your Rights

You have the right to access the information we hold about you, request corrections, and withdraw consent for data collection or processing where applicable. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Commitment to Privacy

Our dedication to protecting your privacy underscores our commitment to delivering exceptional services in a secure and trustworthy environment. For any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this policy, feel free to reach out to us via email, phone, or in person at Ebenezer St Thomas School.
