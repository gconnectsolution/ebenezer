A Legacy of Excellence Since 1993.

Nelmangala’s Best School, Dedicated to Quality Education and Holistic Growth.

Our Foundation

Ebenezer St. Thomas school is the best school in Nelmangala dedicated to delivering quality education with high standards. Our vibrant and inclusive community was officially founded in 1993.

From the beginning, our mission has been simple: to create an environment where every child feels empowered. We believe in discovering and nurturing every child's unique talent, laying the groundwork for confident, capable future leaders.

Our Guiding Motto: “In God we trust” guides our mission to empower students to reach their full potential.

The Global-Ready Curriculum

The curriculum is carefully framed by experts to promote essential skills and knowledge which meet the present global needs. It is the perfect blend of international as well as national syllabus, ensuring comprehensive intellectual growth.

Future-Proof Skills

Promoting critical thinking, problem-solving, and adaptability.

Decision-Making Mastery

Teaching techniques build decision-making ability to face any kind of challenge.

Holistic Nurturing

We provide the best and ideal learning environment with excellent infrastructure to nourish and flourish the future of every student. We take full responsibility to care for and nurture students from pre-primary to Xth.

Overall Development

With a rich history of academic excellence, innovation, and service, we ensure our students have an overall development leading to becoming efficient future professionals tomorrow.

Our Faculty and Vocation

Our experienced faculty and staff are the backbone of the Ebenezer experience. They provide a supportive and stimulating environment, fostering creativity, teamwork, and leadership inside and outside the classroom.

Continuous training and a passion for innovative teaching ensure that every child receives guidance from educators who are dedicated to their success and growth.
